ABOUT OUR STUDIO



Eleven Notes Studio is a small recording studio located in Newroad, Kathmandu, Nepal, run by myself; musician,producer, designer & lecturer Smriti Manandhar. I began my career as a musician, which lead me to releasing many albums and touring internationally in over 25 countries with bands such as Albatross, 1074 AD and many more .

During this period, I worked professionally as a graphic and web designer. But it was on my musical adventures that my love for recording studio enviroment & sound production grew. With a strong vision as a songwriter and musician, it was only a matter of time before sound production would become my first love, and I would seek formal education in this field.This is 12,0000 sq.foot, 3-story, historic buildinging houses a full lime music store . On the main floor, we provide a professional full service recording studio, running ProTools HD and featuring a Yamaha c7 and a grand piano on the main recording floor.

Our goal is to present the real, precised and accurate information about our studio rather than bragging about everything that we have.Also to provide friendy environment, expert guidance and help you start your musical journey. We would try out best to provide you with the best . With over 10 years experience, we know quite a lot about pianos an we will help you with all your piano and keyboard needs.
